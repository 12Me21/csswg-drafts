Long description for floating boxes and margins

This diagram illustrates how floating boxes interact with margins.

It shows the rendering of some image and floating text, surronded by margins. First, there is a top caption indicating that the margin value is "max (BODY margin, P margin)", then below and from left to right: an indication of the presense of a BODY margin, a P margin, the page content itself as an image included in its own box surrounded by its own IMG margins, with text floating to the right and below the image box, then a P margin again and a BODY margin to the far right.
