This diagram illustrates two clipping regions.

Two viewports are shown. Both are graduated 5px by 5px and extent to (50,55) in size. The top window (containing block) shows a content box completely included in the viewport, extending from 10,10 to 40,45. The bottom window shows a content box that extend outside its containing block to the right, starting at 10,10 and ending at 55,45.
