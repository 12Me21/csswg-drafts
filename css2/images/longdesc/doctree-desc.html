Long description for Sample document tree diagram

This diagram illustrates a sample document tree.

The root node (HTML) is at the top, it has two children, HEAD and BODY, HEAD has a TITLE child node, BODY has a H1, a P, and a UL, and the UL has 3 LI leaf nodes.
