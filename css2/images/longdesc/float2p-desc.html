Long description for floating boxes and overlap

This diagram illustrates a floating image that overlaps the borders of two paragraphs: the borders are interrupted by the image.

There are two paragraphs, one on top each other, both with a red line border. An image appears floating on the left bottom part of the top paragraph, extending to the top left portion of the bottom paragraph. The image has a margin space which lets thru the paragraph borders, while the image itself obscures them (i.e. the red border touches the image).
