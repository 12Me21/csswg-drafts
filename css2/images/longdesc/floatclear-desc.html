Long description for floating boxes and clear

This diagram illustrates a floating image and the effect of 'clear: left' on the two paragraphs.

There are two paragraphs, one on top each other, both with a red line border. An image appears floating on the left bottom part of the top paragraph, extending to the top border of the bottom paragraph, which appears pushed down to leave room for the image (no overlap there). The image itself has a margin space on all side which lets thru the top paragraph border, while the bottom paragraph border is right outside the bottom image margin.
