The normative version of the CSS2 Recommendation is in English. If English is not your first language, we plan to provide translations in other languages. If you are in a position to help with this, please write to www-css-editor@w3.org. We expect you to give us permission to republish the translated version and to allow readers to make copies without restrictions.

Email list for Translators

w3c-translators@w3.org is a new mailing list for people working on translations of W3C specifications such as the CSS2 Recommendation. To subscribe send an email to w3c-translators-request@w3.org with the word subscribe in the subject line (include the word unsubscribe if you want to unsubscribe). The archive for the list is acccessible online.
