22 Kang-Hao (Kenny) Lu

Clarify how border-image applies to table elements with collapsing borders (relation to border conflict resolution)

  1. comment
  2. reply

Added “otherwise the element's borders are invisble and the border image is drawn”.

Proposal for the rest has two options: (A) draw the table borders with invisible ink and then draw the image on top of it; or (B) an image border overrides any other border in the conflict resolution. (The difference is the calculation of the width of each cell.)

Bert/Elika to edit to apply the WG decision: Make interaction of border-image with collapsed tables undefined, recommend not applying it to such tables in L3, expect it to be defined in L4.

Accepted
